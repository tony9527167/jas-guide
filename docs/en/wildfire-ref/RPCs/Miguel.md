# Miguel

![Miguel portrait](/public/jas/RPCs/Miguel.jpg)

Miguel Cordona | $0

> Miguel Cordona is the leader of the rebels in Arulco, in Jagged Alliance 2. He is located in the rebel hideout in Omerta (sector A10) with other rebels including Carlos, Ira, and Dimitri. He is a former election candidate. Miguel Cordona's rebel force has begun to receive off-country funding to purchase arms necessary to engage in small-scale skirmishes. He is found after giving the letter of Enrico Chivaldori to Fatima and following her to the rebel hideout.
