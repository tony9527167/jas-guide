# MP7

The MP7 PDW is a quintessential reminder of the lead that HK continues to take in small arms design.

Pros: Light, High Damage, Quick Firing, Fast Burst Fire, Reliable  
Cons: Hard to Repair

Mag\*Cal: 20 \* 4.46mm  
Weight: 1.5  
DAM: 30  
RNG: 19  
AP: 5/9

Locations:

- K4-1 Orta basement
- escort John couple from Chitzena A2 to Drassen B13, they mail two MP7 as a thank you
