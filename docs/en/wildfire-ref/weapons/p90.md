# P90

This Belgian weapon is one of the most advanced submachine guns in the world and is highly coveted by several special forces.

Pros: High Damage, Quick Firing, Large Magazine  
Cons: Hard to Repair

Mag\*Cal: 50 \* 5.7mm  
Weight: 2.6  
DAM: 30  
RNG: 20  
AP: 5/9

With silencer:

RNG: 13  
AP: 6/10

Locations:

- K4-1
- Bobby Bay
- One of Shadow's default pieces of gear

NOTICE: The silencer for P90 is one of the most rarely accessories in game.
