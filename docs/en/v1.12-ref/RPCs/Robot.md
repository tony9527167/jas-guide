Tip(s)

- It moves with fewer action points and has high marksmanship and strong armor (though the exact value are unknown).
- It cannot pass through sewers or swamps, and it makes loud noise while moving.
- You can control the robot once any of your mercenaries equips the remote control device.

## How to obtain MadLab's Robot

**MadLab** Doctor Kairns : A scientist who betrayed the Queen and went into hiding.

- After a few in-game days, you can find him in sector I11 inside a warehouse. Watch for hints on the game screen.
- There is a switch in the house next to the warehouse, operate it to open the hidden door to the warehouse.
- Bring a video camera and a rifle, then speak to him. He will assemble and provide you with a remote control and a lethal combat Robot in next day.
  - Recommended weapon: a light machine gun, such as FN Minimi or RPK-74, which delivers high damage and has high round capacity
  - Alternative option: a long range and high damage sniper rifle, such as the M24

The parts you need buy from:

- video camera: L12 Sam or F12 Franz
