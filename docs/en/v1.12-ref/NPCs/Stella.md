# Stella

Dr. Margaret "Stella" Trammel

Margaret was the first female to join the ranks of A.I.M., and will be fondly missed.
Besides her hand-fought battles on the combat field, Dr. Trammel will also be remembered for her uphill struggles with the A.I.M.
Fraternal Benefits Association to gain spousal benefits for same sex partners.
She recently opened a small family practice in the Virgin Islands.
