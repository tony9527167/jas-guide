## Description

We can hardly begin to list the many battles Biff Apscott has supposedly taken part in.  
His experience, dedication, and willingness to sacrifice his pension fund have made this agency what it is today.  
In a private moment among warriors, Col. Leon Roachburn said: "Biff is a fearless supervisor. A man without equal."

Additional Notes: Biff is responsible for hiring and training personnel for M.E.R.C. and is a silent business partner.
