## Description

Jim Wallace is one of the few military academy cadets to graduate with honors in every subject.  
It certainly didn't hurt that his father wrote the school's code of conduct.  
A consummate professional and master of automatic weapons, he may be M.E.R.C.'s best man.

Additional Notes: Cougar is a man of firm convictions, but his military upbringing and discipline keep them to himself.
