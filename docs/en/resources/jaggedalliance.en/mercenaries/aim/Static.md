## Description

Years go by, but Static never seems to grow up.  
Due to his experience and reserved nature, Kirk works best under cover of darkness.  
With his mechanical and electronics knowledge, he provides valuable service to his superiors.  
And while he looks like a hippie, peace and love are the last things on his mind.

**Additional Notes:** Like his good friend Spider, Static can't stand insects.
