## Description

Edgar Smorth gave up his motorcycle, leather jacket, and kidney belt to become a full-time mercenary at A.I.M.  
Over the years with us, he expanded his knowledge of car bombs and became a military explosives expert.  
Despite the career change, he has retained his brawling spirit and the belly from his wild biker days.

Additional Notes: Edgar has a talent for reaching inaccessible places deftly, as long as they're not too small or narrow.
