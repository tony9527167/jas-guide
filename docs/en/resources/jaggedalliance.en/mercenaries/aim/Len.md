## Description

Anderson's military career began when he joined the Green Berets barely older than a teenager.  
After honorable service, he came to A.I.M. and fought side by side with the founding members in the Battle of Angetta.  
Len is a strong leader; his service deployments have earned him much respect and recognition.

Additional Notes: In his hometown, the community-active Anderson conducts shooting exercises for the National Rifle Association.
