## Description

Whether it's being invited to tea by British nobility or putting a .38 slug into an unwelcome troublemaker, Sidney does it with poise and dignity.  
He combines upper-class manners with the speed of a gunslinger from the Wild West.  
Years of playing cricket have also given him a much-feared throwing arm.
