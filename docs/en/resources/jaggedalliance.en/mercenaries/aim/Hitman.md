## Description

Hitman's carefree manner and imposing appearance have made him one of the most popular and appreciated mercenaries in the organization.  
Unfortunately, he hasn't taken care of himself lately, and his health and abilities have suffered somewhat.  
Despite several attempts, he has not been able to get his weight problem under control.

Additional Notes: Hitman has repeatedly talked about throwing in the towel and becoming a combat instructor.
