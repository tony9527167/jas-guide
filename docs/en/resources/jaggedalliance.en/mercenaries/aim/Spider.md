## Description

With her military training in trauma care, Dr. Houston provides unparalleled first aid under the harshest conditions.  
In the darkest night or during a raging tropical storm, the wounded can rely on Spider to deliver emergency care quickly, calmly, and effectively.

**Additional Notes:** Don't be fooled by her nickname. It refers to her fear of spiders, not her personality.
