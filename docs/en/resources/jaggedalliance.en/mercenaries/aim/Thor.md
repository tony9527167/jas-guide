## Description

This vegetarian New Age healer possesses remarkable medical knowledge for someone who never set foot inside a university.  
He is also quite skilled at quick, silent killing.  
Born in a suburb of Berlin, he now calls California home, where he lives in the mountains with his newborn son and his ex-wife, Cassandra.
