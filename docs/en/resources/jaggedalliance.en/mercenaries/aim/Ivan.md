## Description

Ivan, a former highly decorated major of the Red Army, joined A.I.M. three years ago as a freelancer.  
Despite significant communication problems in a foreign language, he quickly developed into a mercenary through and through, breaking all success records and setting statistics that probably only he himself can surpass.

Additional Notes: To improve his relationships with superiors, he has enrolled in a beginner's language course.
