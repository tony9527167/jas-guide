## Description

This ex-Ranger and sharpshooter has turned sneaking into an art form.  
Shadow could be lurking right next to you and you wouldn't even notice.  
He could be any snowdrift, sand dune, or bush. Simmons has made it his business to merge with any type of environment for as long as needed.

Additional Notes: Shadow brings his own camouflage gear and wants to make one thing clear from the start: he doesn't share.
