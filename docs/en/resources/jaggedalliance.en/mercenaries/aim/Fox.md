## Description

Originally trained as an emergency room nurse, Fox Guzzman was recently featured in Curves in Uniform, a respected monthly magazine for mercenaries.  
Her article on stopping bleeding on the battlefield was almost as highly praised as her two-page nude portrait.

Additional Notes: Guzzman has been practicing frequently at the shooting range lately and has already made considerable progress.
