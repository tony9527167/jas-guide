## Description

Trevor Colby, Australian prodigy and tinkering genius, can turn a harmless toaster into a deadly weapon.  
He has extensive knowledge of explosives and is always good for a surprise.  
With his penchant for practical jokes, he enjoys devising and setting a trap just as much as watching its perfect operation.

Additional Notes: May be a little inexperienced, but learns quickly.
