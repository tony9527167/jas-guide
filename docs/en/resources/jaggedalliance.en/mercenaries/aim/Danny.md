## Description

Doctor Daniel Quinten is a mountaineer, skydiver, and mobile medical practitioner.  
During Operation Desert Storm, he became famous for parachuting from helicopters into sandy minefields to operate on injured soldiers lying there.  
Even more remarkable: Danny only requested evacuation once the patient was safely out of danger.

Additional Notes: He has always had the traits of a lone wolf.
