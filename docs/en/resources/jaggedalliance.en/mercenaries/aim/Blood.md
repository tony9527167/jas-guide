## Description

Whether jungle warfare or close combat, Blood Hanson is the right man for the job.  
Trained in the art of combat techniques, his ability to throw a combat knife into the back of an enemy's neck is a sight to behold: it appears out of nowhere, sails an incredible distance through the air, and hits its target with deadly accuracy.

Additional Notes: Former member of the African National Congress.
