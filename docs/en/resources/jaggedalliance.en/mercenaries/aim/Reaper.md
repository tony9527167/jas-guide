## Description

Anyone who gets close enough to Carl Sheppards—also known as the assassin—to look him in the face has a good chance of taking their last breath.  
Reaper prides himself on patience. He moves completely silently and sneaks up on his victims unnoticed.  
And he enjoys looking them in the eyes at the moment they die.

Additional Notes: One of his former superiors described him as "potentially too dangerous even for service at A.I.M."
