## Description

The switch from fighting fires to fighting foes has worked out brilliantly for the former firefighter from Warsaw.  
With his mechanical skills, he kept the outdated, underfunded Polish fire department in working order.  
Due to his phenomenal eyesight and stubborn endurance, he is considered a dangerous opponent in enemy ranks.

**Additional Notes:** Gontarski refuses any urine tests.
