## Description

Lt. Ron Higgens was head of the Los Angeles SWAT team when he fell in love with his best sniper, Sgt. Charlene Higgens.  
They managed to keep their relationship secret until a year ago. When they decided to marry, the L.A. police no longer allowed them to work together, so they joined A.I.M.

Additional Notes: He is well organized, experienced, and decisive. Raider is a born leader.
