## Description

Whether repairing sidearms or firing bursts from machine guns, the ambidextrous Victoria Waters works best with her hands.  
Outside her assignments for A.I.M., she spends her free time restoring and selling vintage cars at her own classic auto shop.

**Additional Notes:** Despite constant teasing, she insists on always using the stairs, no matter how tall the building.
