## Description

The lovable Scully Sullivan has long been part of A.I.M.  
He is one of our most distinguished mercenaries and perhaps enjoys the greatest respect among his comrades.  
Scully holds the A.I.M. record for the highest number of combat missions in almost as many countries.  
He is the right man to make short work of the enemy.

Additional Notes: Scully is known for his skill with knives and similar weapons.
