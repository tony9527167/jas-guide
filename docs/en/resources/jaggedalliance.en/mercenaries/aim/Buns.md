## Description

The ultra-clean, proper Monica Sonderguard might give the impression of being unsuited for the rough and restless mercenary life, but she far exceeds the minimum requirements.  
Before A.I.M., she gained experience as a kindergarten teacher, elderly caregiver, Danish Olympic sharpshooter in Atlanta, and professional soldier.
