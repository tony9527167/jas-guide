## Description

Don't be fooled by his blue eyes and boyish looks.  
Rothman holds his own, even when the situation gets critical.  
As a mercenary and security consultant in the diamond industry, he has protected the richest mines in the world from thieving locals, rebels, and competitors.  
South America is said to have gotten rougher since he left.

**Additional Notes:** Stephan has proven to be an excellent troop leader, especially during night maneuvers.
