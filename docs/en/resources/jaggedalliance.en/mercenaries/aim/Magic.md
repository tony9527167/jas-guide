## Description

Magic's calm, cool, and confident manner carries over into his fighting style.  
He is in peak physical condition and displays razor-sharp reflexes and cat-like agility.  
He senses danger with astonishing accuracy. And thanks to his deadly marksmanship, he quickly and effectively eliminates any threat.

Additional Notes: He owes his nickname, Magic, to the ease with which every door seems to open for him.
